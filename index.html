Summary
PT. Eagan Warsana Energi, EAGAN, is a growing medium size Engineering Procurement Construction (EPC) company which is specializing in civil engineering, platform work, pipeline system, steel structure and electrical services. Establised on March 2011, EAGAN has already proven capable of playing a sound role in civil, mechanical and steel structures projects with all project performed in excellent condition thoughout Indonesia
Vision
Our aim is ultimately become a trustworthy and an excellent-leading Engineering Procurement Construction (EPC) company specializing in civil engineer, mechanical electrical and steel structure work with the purspose of giving a customer-statisfaction service to all stakeholders
Mission
  • Utilizing a moderate project management support system to ensure our commitment to the clients in term of budget and schedule
  • Operating a tight company policy and continuous improvement in Quality and Safety
  • Developing an excellent communication system along with the responsible solution to our strategic partners and clients
